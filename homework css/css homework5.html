A Winter Day diary

Angeleena

The Sunlight hidden behind clouds, made my pet roll under carpet…
Sometimes rainy, sometimes cloudy…. sometimes can’t even say…
Fluctuating climatic conditions changed everything!
Tiny wind murmured in ears, the story of old flowers…
Nature contains the history, present and future
Winter makes it brighter and explains its journey with human…
I went out for a walk to find sacredness of wilderness!
Into the less polluted, wet soil fragranced atmosphere…
I skipped using footwear, it stood my feet on soil
Withering yellow leaves remembered suffering!
I felt the pain of those leaves, which once were green…
