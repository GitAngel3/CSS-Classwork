National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM) Scholarship 2025 offers attractive rewards to students who are interested in the field of Mathematics. The scholarship is being offered by the National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM), set up by the Government of India under the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE).Apart from the various regular activities NBHM endeavours to promote mathematics in India, at the advanced level , through various special programmes. The National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM) was set up in the year 1983 to foster the development of higher mathematics in India. It formulates policies for the development of mathematics and extends in the development and establishment of mathematical centres. Financial Support The NBHM has various schemes to provide financial support to institutions and individuals to strengthen the mathematical base/potential through study, exchange or other such programs. The links on the left provide information for the different schemes. The support of NBHM must be acknowledged in all the publications/patents coming out of the financial support of NBHM. Contingency Grant(RP (JRF/SRF), PDF, PhD) can be utilised for the following : Purchase of stationery and books. Books purchased by the scholar from the contingency grant are allowed to be retained by him/her. Travel inside the country for attending conference/lectures, etc. Towards payment for computer, laptop, tab and computer accessories Any other purpose specifically authorised by the department. The unutilized part of the contingency grant is not allowed to be carried forward to the next year.
